个人简介

杨雅君，天津大学人工智能学院副教授/博士生导师。研究方向为：大模型知识增强、社会计算、图计算和图挖掘。目前担任中国计算机学会数据库专业委员会执行委员、信息系统专业委员会执行委员。 分别于哈尔滨工业大学计算机科学与技术学院 和数学与应用数学学院获得博士学位和学士学位，师从李建中教授和高宏教授。曾在香港中文大学系统工程管理系担任和澳大利亚格里菲斯大学信息工程学院担任访问学者。目前在SIGMOD/VLDB/ICDE/KDD/NeurIPS/TOIS/ICDM/CIKM等国际会议和期刊上发表论文40余篇。 主持国家自然基金重点项目课题、面上项目、青年项目、重点研发计划子课题等多项国家级项目。长期担任 CIKM、DASFAA、APWEB-WAIM、ADMA 等国际重要学术会议程序委员会委员，以及担任 TKDE、WWW、Pattern Recognation、Information Science 等国际顶级或重要学术期刊和会议的审稿人。

欢迎有意加入我们团队攻读硕士/博士研究生的同学与我联系，请邮件咨询 yjyang@tju.edu.cn.

About Me

I am currently working as a associate professor in School of Computer Science and Technology at Tianjin University. I am an executive committee member of Technology Committee of Information System and Technology Committee of Database in CCF. I received my Ph.D degree and B.S. degree in School of Computer Science and Technology and Applied Mathematics repectively from Harbin Institute of Technology. I worked as a visiting scholar in Chinese Universtiy of Hong Kong and Griffith Unviersity. Now I am a PC member of DASFAA, APWEB-WAIM, ICPCSEE, ADMA, and invited reviewer of TKDE, WWW, Pattern Recognation, Information Science, ICPP and so on.

Welcome to join us, if you are interested in graph data management, graph mining, machine learning and social network analysis.
